Research
My Research focuses on solving a long time problem in radio astronomy, the association of AGN components in an automated way. This association is key for finding optical counterparts to radio sources and thus understanding the physics of the radio source. So far this research has focused on source detection, to find components of these radio sources to then feed into a machine learning solution. This research is being conducted in preparation for the SKA obervatory (due for completion after 2027) where the number of radio sources will massively increase.
If you want to talk to be about my research please send me an email.
